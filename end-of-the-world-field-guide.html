METHOD · Picks parsed and attributed per contributor across 178 weekly sheets. To keep curations from drowning out theme picks, an artist counts once per week it appears for a person, regardless of track count — so a 32-song Wire curation and a single Wire theme pick weigh the same. Top-artist lists rank by number of distinct weeks; node size is distinct artists; overlap is shared artists with single-week co-curations (e.g. Rob & Adrian on Big Star, Kara's Tropicália) excluded. Combined credits are counted for each named member. Characterizations are read off actual picks, not external genre data.
